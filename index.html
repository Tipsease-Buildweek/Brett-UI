Why us?

The problem

Servers depend on your tips for their survival. If you don't tip, they don't make any money. Even if you do, you never know if that money really goes to your server.
In some restaurants, servers have to share large portions of their tips. They may only get a small percentage of what you give them.

What about me?

Our mission is to help our food industry workers and help you!
We know how bothersome it can be to look through your wallet for bills or change. We make it easy to calculate your tips and allow you to pay your server what they deserve with no stress.

Our Guarantee

With Tipsease, we make all your tipping problems go away.
You feel better, your server feels better, and we feel better!
